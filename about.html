Fox Pathway

What is Fox Pathway?


Fox Pathway is a short quiz based on your associations. Japanese Kanji are best learnt by seeing them as pictures. The quiz takes your images of the pictures first. Then it asks you to remember your picture in a single-choice style quiz.

What do I do in each section?


In the first section, you see one basic Kanji, a Japanese meaning-bearing character. You write what you see in the text field and submit. After ten characters, you are presented with the quiz part. Just click the correct solution, you can then see your score at the end.
